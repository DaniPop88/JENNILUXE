Privacy Policy

At JENNILUXE HOME MART, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to safeguarding your personal information and ensuring your shopping experience is secure, confidential, and respectful.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when you place an order, sign up for updates, or contact us. This may include your name, address, phone number, email, and payment details. We only gather what is necessary to serve you better and fulfill your orders.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process orders, arrange delivery, and communicate updates
  • To respond to inquiries, provide customer support, and improve our services
  • To send you notifications about your purchases, offers, or important updates (only when you opt in)

Data Protection & Security

Your personal information is stored securely and accessed only by authorized staff. We use industry-standard safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. We do not sell or share your information with third parties, except as needed to process your order (such as with delivery partners).

Your Rights

  • You may request to review, update, or delete your personal information at any time
  • You can opt out of marketing communications whenever you wish
  • If you have questions about how your data is managed, contact us for assistance

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and help us improve our services. Cookies are small files stored on your device that remember your preferences and help streamline shopping. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or the law. We encourage you to check this page regularly. Significant updates will be communicated on our website.
